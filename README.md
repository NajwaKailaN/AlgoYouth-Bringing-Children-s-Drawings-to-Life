# AlgoYouth: Bringing Children's Drawings to Life

## Overview

AlgoYouth is an interactive educational platform designed to make learning more engaging, creative, and meaningful for children.

Developed in collaboration with **RAIA Kids Club**, this project introduces children to the biodiversity of the **Kerinci Rainforest** through an immersive learning journey. Children explore native flora and fauna, create their own drawings, and watch their artwork come to life as animated rainforest characters.

By combining environmental education, storytelling, and technology, AlgoYouth transforms children from passive learners into active creators.

---

## Problem Statement

Traditional learning methods often rely on one-way information delivery, making it difficult for children to stay engaged and express their creativity.

In addition, some children face challenges communicating their thoughts and ideas verbally.

We asked ourselves:

> How can technology help children learn, imagine, and express themselves in a more meaningful way?

---

## Our Solution

AlgoYouth allows children to:

1. Learn about Kerinci Rainforest biodiversity.
2. Draw their favorite flora and fauna.
3. Upload or scan their drawings.
4. Watch their creations appear inside a digital rainforest environment.
5. Interact with a living ecosystem built from their own imagination.

This creates an educational experience that is interactive, memorable, and inclusive.

---

## Key Features

### Biodiversity Learning

Introduce children to native flora and fauna found in the Kerinci Rainforest.

### Interactive Drawing Experience

Children can create their own rainforest creatures and plants.

### Drawing Recognition

The system recognizes children's drawings using computer vision techniques.

### Animated Rainforest Environment

Drawings are transformed into animated digital characters.

### Visual Storytelling

Encourages creativity, imagination, and self-expression through interactive storytelling.
